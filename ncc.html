About USTM NCC

USTM NCC works to develop discipline, leadership, teamwork, confidence and national responsibility among cadets. Through regular drill, theory classes, camps, adventure activities, social service and institutional events, cadets learn to serve with commitment and pride.

NCC Activities

Drill Training

Cadets are trained in discipline, parade movements, command response and military bearing.

Theory Classes

Classes include leadership, personality development, map reading, field craft and NCC knowledge.

Camps

Cadets participate in CATC, trekking, firing, adventure camps and national level activities.

Social Service

Awareness drives, blood donation, cleanliness activities and community service are key parts of NCC.

Achievements

  • Participation in NCC camps, university events and institutional duties.
  • Cadets selected for adventure, trekking, firing and map reading activities.
  • Active contribution in discipline management and volunteer coordination at USTM events.
  • Development of leadership, confidence, teamwork and public responsibility among cadets.
